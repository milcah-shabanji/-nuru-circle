Our story

Built by a parent who has lived it

"I had no map. No clear first step. No one who truly understood what I was facing."

Nine years ago, I was handed a diagnosis that changed my world. My son was one year old, diagnosed with autism following a condition called ventriculomegaly during my pregnancy.

I figured it out slowly, painfully, and with a great deal of love. My son now walks, socialises, and attends an integrated school. He turns ten in December.

I am also a marketing and community professional. I have spent my career building health and parenting communities. Nuru Circle is what I wished had existed when I started this journey, built so no Kenyan family ever has to feel alone again.

Nuru means light in Swahili. This is yours.
